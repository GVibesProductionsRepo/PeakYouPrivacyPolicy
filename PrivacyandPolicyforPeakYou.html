Privacy Policy for PeakYou Self-Development App

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how PeakYou, developed by G-Vibe and owned by Hamza Khan, handles data in the self-development Android app.

Data Usage

Async Storage: We utilize async storage on your device to save your app-related data, such as meditation session settings, to-do lists, journal entries, and addiction recovery progress. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Eligibility for 13-Year-Olds

PeakYou is suitable for 13-year-old users because it promotes self-development, mindfulness, and personal growth. We do not collect any personal data from minors.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement measures to protect the data stored on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in app functionality. You will be notified of any significant updates, and your use of the app implies your acceptance of the revised policy.
